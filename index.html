FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is this different from my CRM's email tool?+

Your CRM sends what you tell it. Revio decides who is worth contacting, diagnoses why each lead went cold, ranks them by recoverable dollars, and drafts the message. The scoring is computed in code — reproducible and auditable, not a black box.

Do I need technical skills?+

No. Upload a CSV or connect your CRM, pick your industry, run a scan. The command center fills with ranked, revivable leads in minutes.

Is the outreach compliant?+

Revio is built around consent and suppression: it tracks lawful basis per lead, honors opt-outs instantly, and by default requires a human to approve every message before it sends — keeping you on the right side of CAN-SPAM, TCPA and GDPR.

How fast will I see results?+

Your first scan runs in minutes. Most teams find revivable revenue on day one and book their first recovered calls within the first week.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Every plan starts with a 14-day trial and you can cancel anytime from billing.
